Indicativo presente

is similar to the simple present tense in English grammar. We use this to tense to speak about actions in the present and near future. It describes routine or repeated actions as well as permanent situations.

Usage

Ways of use

We use the Spanish simple present tense for:

facts or conditions in the present
actions in the present
to express how long something has been going on
future actions, but only with a corresponding indication of the future time
